## Signals that a posting is worth your time

- "Learning design" OR "instructional design" in title (not just "training")
- "Needs analysis" or "evaluation" in responsibilities
- Band 4–6 equivalent or $85,000+ posted range
- "Hybrid" or "remote" explicitly stated
- Portfolio or case studies requested
- "Systems" or "strategy" language in the description
- Located at or connected to a Tier 1 or Tier 2 org

## Red flags in postings

- Title is "Coordinator" with no "Senior" or "Specialist" qualifier
- Salary below $75,000
- "Facilitate existing programs" as the primary responsibility
- Heavy Articulate/Storyline/SCORM e-learning focus
- No mention of design, needs analysis, or evaluation
- "Fast-paced environment" with no equity or wellbeing language

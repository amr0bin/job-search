## Professional summary

Learning design specialist with fifteen+ years of practice across higher education, research institutions, and the public sector. Designs learning systems at the organizational level — diagnosing what prevents learning from working, building conditions for change, and integrating equity and accessibility as structural design practices rather than compliance requirements. Background in neurobiology, visual arts, and public health grounds an approach that is evidence-based, systems-oriented, and built for sustainability.

# Sector vocabulary guide

The same work described in the wrong language gets filtered out before a human reads it.
This guide translates your practice into each sector's native vocabulary.

---

## Your core concepts and how to say them

| Your concept | Higher education | BC public sector | Research institutions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Learning systems design | Curriculum architecture / instructional design | Organizational learning strategy | Knowledge mobilization / training design |
| Needs analysis | Learning needs assessment / program review | Training needs analysis / gap analysis | Capacity assessment |
| Equity integration | Universal Design for Learning / inclusive pedagogy | Equity, diversity & inclusion in learning | Accessible knowledge transfer |
| Evaluation framework | Program evaluation / learning outcomes assessment | Training effectiveness measurement | Research impact / knowledge translation |
| Facilitation | Workshop facilitation / pedagogical practice | Learning facilitation / capacity building | Knowledge exchange facilitation |
| Systems thinking | Curriculum mapping / program coherence | Organizational change / LMS strategy | Research dissemination strategy |
| Stakeholder co-design | Curriculum consultation / faculty development | Engagement / change management | Participatory research / co-creation |

## Higher education

**Titles to target:** Instructional Designer · Learning Design Specialist · Curriculum Developer · Educational Developer · Teaching & Learning Specialist · Centre for Teaching & Learning roles

**Key vocabulary:** pedagogy · andragogy · learning outcomes · constructive alignment · academic integrity · faculty development · student success · accessibility · UDL · Bloom's taxonomy · authentic assessment · experiential learning

**What they care about:** Faculty buy-in · student outcomes · accreditation standards · accessibility compliance · evidence-based practice

**Tone:** Academic but accessible. Reference theory by name. Show that you understand faculty culture and the difference between support and prescription.

**Institutions in Vancouver/BC:** UBC · SFU · BCIT · Langara · Capilano · Emily Carr · Vancouver Community College · Kwantlen Polytechnic · Royal Roads · Thompson Rivers · Justice Institute of BC

## BC public sector

**Titles to target:** Learning & Development Specialist · Organizational Learning Consultant · Training Designer · Curriculum Developer · Workforce Development Specialist · Senior Advisor, Learning

**Key vocabulary:** capacity building · workforce development · change management · knowledge transfer · competency frameworks · onboarding · compliance training · performance support · blended learning · LMS · HRIS

**What they care about:** Scalability · consistency · compliance · cost-effectiveness · accessibility (AODA/WCAG equivalent) · union considerations · classification systems

**Tone:** Clear, outcomes-focused, government-adjacent language. Reference prior public sector experience (ICBC, BC work). Avoid jargon-heavy academic framing — translate to plain language.

**Key employers:** BC Public Service Agency · WorkSafeBC · ICBC · BC Housing · BC Centre for Disease Control · Fraser Health · Vancouver Coastal Health · Translink · Metro Vancouver

## Research institutions

**Titles to target:** Learning Design Specialist · Education & Outreach Specialist · Knowledge Mobilization Specialist · Training & Development Lead · Research Training Coordinator · Education Program Manager

**Key vocabulary:** knowledge mobilization · research translation · capacity building · outreach · science communication · researcher training · graduate education · professional development · evaluation · logic models

**What they care about:** Research integrity · communicating complex concepts · training early-career researchers · public engagement · impact measurement · grant accountability

**Tone:** Intellectually rigorous, but not pretentious. Show systems thinking and evaluation orientation. Reference your TRIUMF work naturally — it signals you understand research culture.

**Key employers in BC:** TRIUMF · MITACS · Genome BC · Michael Smith Genome Sciences Centre · BC Cancer · BCCDC · SFU research centres · UBC research institutes · Hakai Institute

## Cultural and knowledge organizations

**Titles to target:** Learning Programs Manager · Education Specialist · Public Programs Designer · Interpretive Planner · Learning Experience Designer · Visitor Experience Designer

**Key vocabulary:** public programming · interpretive design · visitor experience · community engagement · accessible design · youth programming · informal learning · community of practice

**What they care about:** Public accessibility · diverse audiences · community relationships · creative programming · engagement metrics · grant reporting

**Tone:** Warm, community-oriented, creative. Lead with your visual arts and public health background more than in other sectors. Science World is a former employer — this is a warm door.

**Key employers:** Science World · Vancouver Art Gallery · Museum of Anthropology · Royal BC Museum · Beaty Biodiversity Museum · Vancouver Public Library · ArtStarts · Pacific Museum of Earth
